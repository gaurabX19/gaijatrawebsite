Introduction

"Gaijatra ( Locally named as "Saparu") is a unique carnival rejoiced in Kathmandu valley by Newari people. On this day people commemorate all those who passed away during the years. The family and relatives go for procession round the city with a cow or the one decorated like a cow (Locally known as "Snacha")".
